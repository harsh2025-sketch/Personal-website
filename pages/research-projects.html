Novel Learning Paradigms for Artificial General Intelligence: Beyond Deep Learning Architectures

2025 Independent Research • IIT Madras In Progress

Abstract

This research explores breakthrough approaches to artificial general intelligence through innovative learning paradigms that transcend current deep learning limitations. We investigate meta-learning architectures, causal reasoning frameworks, and emergent intelligence systems that can adapt and generalize across diverse domains without extensive retraining. The work focuses on developing unified knowledge representation schemes and cognitive architectures that mirror human-like reasoning capabilities while maintaining computational efficiency.

Key contributions include: (1) A novel meta-learning framework for cross-domain knowledge transfer, (2) Causal reasoning integration in neural architectures, (3) Emergent behavior analysis in multi-agent AGI systems, and (4) Ethical alignment mechanisms for AGI development.

Keywords: Artificial General Intelligence, Meta-Learning, Causal Reasoning, Emergent Intelligence, Cognitive Architectures

Hyper-Personalized Financial Intelligence: Real-time Adaptive Advisory Systems Using Advanced ML

2024 Frontier AI Research • IIT Madras Published

Abstract

Traditional financial advisory systems suffer from scalability limitations and inability to adapt to rapidly changing market conditions. This research presents a comprehensive framework for hyper-personalized financial intelligence that integrates real-time market data, individual behavioral patterns, and advanced risk assessment algorithms to provide personalized financial advisory services at scale.

Our approach combines reinforcement learning for dynamic portfolio optimization, natural language processing for sentiment analysis from financial news and social media, and graph neural networks for modeling complex financial relationships. The system demonstrates superior performance compared to traditional robo-advisors and human advisors in terms of risk-adjusted returns and client satisfaction metrics.

Keywords: Financial AI, Personalized Advisory, Reinforcement Learning, Risk Assessment, Behavioral Finance

Multimodal Healthcare Diagnostics: Integrating Medical Imaging, Genomics, and Clinical Data for Precision Medicine

2024 Chandigarh University • Healthcare AI Lab Published

Abstract

Healthcare diagnostics traditionally rely on isolated data sources, leading to suboptimal diagnostic accuracy and treatment personalization. This research develops an integrated multimodal AI system that combines medical imaging (CT, MRI, X-ray), genomic sequencing data, electronic health records, and clinical laboratory results to provide comprehensive diagnostic support for healthcare professionals.

The system employs advanced deep learning architectures including vision transformers for medical image analysis, graph convolutional networks for genomic data processing, and attention mechanisms for temporal clinical data analysis. Clinical validation across multiple hospitals demonstrates significant improvements in diagnostic accuracy, particularly for complex cases requiring interdisciplinary analysis.

Keywords: Healthcare AI, Multimodal Learning, Medical Imaging, Genomics, Precision Medicine

Context-Aware Language Models for Cross-Domain Knowledge Transfer and Reasoning

2023 IIT Madras • Natural Language Processing Lab Published

Abstract

Current language models excel at pattern recognition but struggle with true understanding and cross-domain reasoning. This research introduces novel architectural improvements to transformer-based models that enhance contextual understanding and enable effective knowledge transfer across disparate domains. The work focuses on developing attention mechanisms that can maintain long-term contextual memory and reasoning capabilities.

Our contributions include a new attention architecture that incorporates causal reasoning, a knowledge distillation framework for cross-domain transfer, and evaluation metrics that better capture understanding versus memorization. The models demonstrate superior performance on reasoning benchmarks and show improved generalization to unseen domains.

Keywords: Natural Language Processing, Transformers, Knowledge Transfer, Causal Reasoning, Contextual Understanding
