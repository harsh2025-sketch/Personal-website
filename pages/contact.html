Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common inquiries

What types of collaboration opportunities are you open to?

I'm interested in research partnerships, co-founding ventures, consulting projects, advisory roles, and speaking engagements. I'm particularly excited about collaborations that advance AGI research or apply AI to solve real-world problems in healthcare and finance.

Are you available for consulting or freelance projects?

Yes, I offer consulting services for AI strategy, technical development, and research guidance. I'm selective about projects to ensure I can provide high-quality work that aligns with my expertise and interests.

How can investors learn more about Frontier AI?

Please reach out via email or the contact form above with "Investment Opportunity" as the subject. I'll be happy to share our pitch deck and schedule a detailed discussion about our vision, technology, and funding needs.

Do you accept speaking engagement requests?

I'm always interested in sharing insights about AI research and the future of AGI. Please provide details about your event, audience, timeline, and topic preferences when reaching out.

What's the best way to stay updated with your work?

Follow me on LinkedIn for professional updates and GitHub for code releases. You can also subscribe to updates through the contact form to receive occasional newsletters about major developments.

How quickly do you typically respond to messages?

I aim to respond to all messages within 48 hours. For urgent matters, please indicate this in your subject line. Complex inquiries may require additional time for a thoughtful response.
